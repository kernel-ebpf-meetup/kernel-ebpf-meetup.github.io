What We Discuss

Our topics are driven by community interest through surveys. We cover a wide range of subjects including:

  • Linux kernel internals and development
  • eBPF programming and use cases
  • Performance monitoring and tracing
  • Networking and XDP
  • Security and sandboxing with eBPF
  • Kernel debugging techniques
  • Latest kernel features and patches
  • Real-world production experiences

üìä Your Voice Matters! Topics are collected through community surveys. We want to discuss what YOU want to learn and share.

Meetup Format

We keep things relaxed and flexible to encourage genuine learning and networking:

  • Casual atmosphere - No formal presentations unless you want to!
  • Open discussions - Share experiences, ask questions, learn together
  • Networking - Connect with other kernel and eBPF developers
  • Hands-on - Bring your laptop for impromptu coding sessions
  • All levels welcome - From curious beginners to kernel maintainers
